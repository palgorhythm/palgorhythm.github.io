polyrhythm at BPM
Fractal Polyrhythm: a composite polyrhythm in which the ratio between every two subsequent voices is the same.
You can calculate the ratio between any two voices in a FP by taking the numbers in the generating ratio to the power of the distance in levels between the two voices. For example, in a 3-level 2:3 polyrhythm, we have four voices, and suppose their names are A, B, C, and D. The ratio between A and B, B and C, and C and D are all 2:3, which makes the ratio between A and C 4:9, and B and D 4:9. The ratio between A and D is now 8:27.
